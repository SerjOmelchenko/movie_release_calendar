Welcome to Movie Release Radar

Your personal calendar for upcoming movie releases worldwide. Here's a quick tour of what you can do.

📅
Browse the Release Calendar

Navigate months with the ← → arrows.

Click any day with movies to see what's releasing. If a day has more than one release you'll get a picker — choose a title to read its synopsis, rating, and genres.

🌍
Filter by Country

Use the Region dropdown to see only movies releasing in your country. Your choice is remembered the next time you visit.

♥
Save Movies to Your Watchlist

Hover over any movie poster and click ♡ to save it. Open your Watchlist anytime from the top bar to review saved titles sorted by release date. Everything is stored locally in your browser.
